ANTI-COVID-19 measures

Classes will be in hybrid modality, which means that you can come to class or join remotely
We have taken lots of measures to ensure safe learning in the classroom. Here are the measures:


Facemasks

  • You must wear a mask when you enter the BrightSpace building and when you walk around in the communal areas
  • Once you’re seated in the classroom, you can take off your masks. Facemasks are not obligatory in class, but welcomed

Sanitization

  • Sanitize your hands when you enter the building. There’s a sanitizing station at the entrance
  • You will find hand sanitizer at your desk. Please, use it everytime you sit down or you leave your desk
  • Desks will be sanitized before and after each class

Classroom ventilation

  • The class will be ventilated before and after class and during breaks
  • If the weather allows, we will keep the windows open during class

Number of people and distance

  • In the classroom there can be a maximum of 8 students and 1 teacher
  • Desks are at least 1.5 meters apart
  • Keep at least 1.5 meter distance from others in and around the building

Symptoms

  • In you have any symptoms, you are required to stay at home and join online
  • If you have been in contact with any person with covid-19, you are required to stay at home and join online

If you have any questions, contact us
