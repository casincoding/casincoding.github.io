Private Lessons

One-to-one sessions to learn python, improve your programming skills, and become a data science expert



Your goals

  • Learning to code for personal and professional growth - see our courses
  • Successfully passing a programming exam in python
  • Getting support while attending an online courses
  • Getting support for computational projects



What you will learn

  • Python language and computational thinking
  • Scientific python: Numpy, Pandas, Keras, Juptyer notebook, and more
  • Visualization packages: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Altair, and more



What I will create for you

  • Personalized learning path
  • Clear explanations and structured knowledge
  • Customized examples and exercises



How it works

  • We will have a first 20 min free lesson to discuss your goals and define the path to reach them
  • If you want to learn coding, I will prepare Jupyter notebooks with explanation and exercises, and we will work on them together during class. I will also prepare assignments for you so that you can exercise between sessions
  • If you want support for projects, we will work together on code and design to make your project successful. I will also provide you tips and tricks that you will be able to use independently for your future projects
  • Sessions will be on Skype. Hours are from 9:00 to 19:00 Central European Time, from Monday to Friday (exceptionally on Saturdays)
