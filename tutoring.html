Tutoring

One-to-one lessons to learn python, improve your programming skills, and become a data science expert



Your goals

  • Successfully passing a programming exam in python
  • Getting support during your thesis or computational project
  • Getting support while attending an online courses
  • Learning to code for personal and professional growth



What you will learn

  • Python language and computational thinking
  • Scientific python: Numpy, Pandas, Keras, Juptyer notebook, and more
  • Visualization packages: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Altair, and more



What I will create for you

  • Personalized learning path
  • Clear explanations and structured knowledge
  • Customized examples and exercises



How it works

  • We will have a first 20 min free lesson to discuss your goals and define the path to reach them
  • I will prepare Jupyter notebooks with explanation and exercises, and we will work on them together during class
  • If you want, I will prepare specific assignments for you
  • Classes will be on Skype. Hours are from 9:00 to 17:00 Central European Time, from Monday to Friday (exceptionally on Saturdays)
